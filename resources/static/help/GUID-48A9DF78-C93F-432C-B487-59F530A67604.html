Discovery overview

You can discover new resources or existing resources that are already configured within your environment. After discovery, you can deploy services on these resources from a template.

The Discover Resources wizard enables you to discover resources. To access the wizard, perform either of the following actions:

  • On the menu bar, click Resources. On the Resources page, click Discover on the All Resources tab.
  • On the Getting Started page, click Discover Resources.
