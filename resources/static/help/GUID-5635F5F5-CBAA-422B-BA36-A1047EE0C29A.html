Editing default time zone and NTP settings

Editing these settings changes the default time zone and NTP nodes applied to VxFlex Manager. All time data is stored in UTC format, and is used to display log and event time stamps.

  1. On the menu bar, click Settings and then click Virtual Appliance Management.

  2. On the Virtual Appliance Management page, under the Time Zone and NTP Settings section, click Edit.

  3. From the Time Zone drop-down list, select a time zone.

  4. Enter the IP address or hostname in Preferred NTP Node and Secondary NTP Node (optional) for time synchronization.

  5. Click Save. VxFlex Manager becomes unavailable because the web services are restarted, the virtual appliance shell is still accessible, and all active users are logged out.
