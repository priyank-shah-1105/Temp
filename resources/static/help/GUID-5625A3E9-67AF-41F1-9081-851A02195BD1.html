Cleaning up ESXi cluster deployments

If you delete the service selecting all the components in the deployment, ASM turns off the ESXi hosts and deletes any of the objects and connectivity created during the deployment. However, ASM will not remove the data center and VDSs in vCenter because those may be shared resources.
