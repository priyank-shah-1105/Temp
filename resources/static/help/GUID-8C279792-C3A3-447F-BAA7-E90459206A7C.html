Editing credentials

To edit a credential:

  1. On the menu bar, click Settings and then click Credentials Management.

  2. On the Credential Management page, click a credential that you want to edit, and click Edit.

  3. Modify the credential information in the Edit Credentials dialog box.

  4. Click Save.
