How to perform common tasks: Monitoring system health

Once VxFlex Manager is configured, you can then use it to perform the ongoing tasks necessary to maintain your VxRack FLEX.

The following table describes typical tasks for monitoring system health and managing RCM compliance and what steps to take in VxFlex Manager to initiate each.

If you want to...Do this in VxFlex Manager...
Monitor system resources and healthOn the Dashboard, look at the Service Overview and Resource Overview sections.
Monitor RCM complianceClick Services. On the Services page, select a service. On the Details page, under Service Actions, click View Compliance Report.
Perform RCM remediationFrom the compliance report, view the firmware or software components. Click Update Resources to update non-compliant resources.
Generate a troubleshooting bundleClick Settings and then click Virtual Appliance Management. Click Generate Troubleshooting Bundle.
