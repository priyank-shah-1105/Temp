Updating the inventory for a service

When new resources are added or removed outside of VxFlex Manager, you can update the inventory for a service to pull in those changes. Updating the inventory will not work for nodes removed externally.

You can update the inventory for a new service that was deployed with VxFlex Manager, or for an existing service that includes resources that were not originally deployed with VxFlex Manager.

Whenever you update the inventory, VxFlex Manager pulls in a representation of the components from the vCenter cluster or VxFlex OS Gateway that was specified for the service. This might include new nodes, volumes, or SVMs. When a new component is added to the inventory for a service, VxFlex Manager does not modify the component.

  1. On the menu bar, click Services.

  2. On the Services page, click the service, and in the right pane, click View Details.

  3. On the Service Details page, in the right pane, under Service Actions, click Update Inventory.

  4. Review the Inventory Summary page and click Next.

  5. Review the OS Credentials page, and click Next.

  6. Review the Summary page, and click Finish.

    VxFlex Manager checks the configuration and inventory of selected resources to be sure that everything is configured properly. The service is then moved to the In Progress state, and any new component types appear on the Service Details page. In addition, the Recent Activity shows that the new deployment has started.
