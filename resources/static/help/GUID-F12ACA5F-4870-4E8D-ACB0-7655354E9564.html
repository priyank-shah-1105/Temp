Editing a template

You can edit an existing template to change its draft state to published for deployment, or to modify its components and their properties.

To edit a template:

  1. On the menu bar, click Templates.

  2. Select a template, and click Edit in the right pane.

  3. To edit template information, click the edit icon in the Template Information section. Make the necessary changes and click Save.

  4. Click Publish Template to make the template ready for deployment
