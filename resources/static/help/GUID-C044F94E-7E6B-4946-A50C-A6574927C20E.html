Editing the alert connector settings

You can edit the connector, OpenManage Enterprise, and registration settings. You can also temporarily suspend alerts for a specific time period and deregister the alert connector.

When the alert connector is initially configured, nodes in the OpenManage Enterprise inventory are automatically enabled to send critical and error alerts for node and VxFlex OS level events to Secure Remote Services (SRS). If the environment changes (resources are expanded or changed), you must configure nodes for the alert connector to enable alerting on any of the resources that expanded or changed. If the nodes are not configured for the alert connector after resources are expanded or changed, SRS does not receive critical or error alerts for those resources.

  1. Click Settings from the menu, and then click Virtual Appliance Management.

  2. Go to Alert Connector, click Edit, and change the settings as required.

    • To temporarily suspend sending alerts to SRS, click Edit to the right of the State field. You can choose to suspend the alerts for 3, 6, 12 hours or 24 hours. You can restart the sending of alerts at any time by clicking Enabled. The state and time that is displayed is based on hours selected for suspension and the NTP node settings.
    • To deregister the alert connector and stop the collection and sending of alerts to SRS, click Deregister. The State of the alert connector changes.
