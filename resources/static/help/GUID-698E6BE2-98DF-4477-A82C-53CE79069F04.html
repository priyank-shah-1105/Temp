Cancelling a service

Cancelling a service stops a scheduled/pending, or in-process service deployment. After cancelling a service, you can update the content and retry the deployment again later.

To cancel a service:

  1. On the menu bar, click Services.

  2. Select a service that is in In Progress or Pending state.

  3. On the Service Details page, in the right pane, click Cancel.

  4. On the Cancel Deployment page, click Cancel Deployment.

    You cannot cancel existing services and the services used for managing the firmware.

    When cancelling a deployment, a banner pops up at the top of the screen that displays: Warning: This service is in the process of being cancelled. During cancellation this service will continue to be updated to reflect activity.
