Service states

The following table describes the available service states.

Service stateIconDescription
HealthyIndicates that service was deployed successfully and the resources are firmware complaint and healthy.
WarningIndicates that the one of more resources that are part of a service is in a state that requires corrective action, but does not affect overall system health. For example, the firmware running on the resource is not at the required level or not compliant.
CriticalIndicates that service deployment has failed due to some issues.
PendingIndicates that the deployment is scheduled for a later time or date.
In ProgressIndicates that service deployment is in progress.
CancelledIndicates that the service deployment was cancelled.
IncompleteIndicates that the service is not fully functional because it has no volumes associated with it. Click Add Resources to add volumes.
Service ModeIndicates that the service has been placed in service mode because one of the nodes within the service was put into service mode.
