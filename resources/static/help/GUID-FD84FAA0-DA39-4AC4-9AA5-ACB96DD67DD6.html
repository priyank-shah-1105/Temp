User roles

When you log in to VxFlex Manager, what you see and what you can do varies depending on your assigned user role.

VxFlex Manager provides three levels of user roles:

  • Administrators have the highest level of access in VxFlex Manager, with the ability to create, modify, and delete all objects in VxFlex Manager.
  • Standard users can view pages and perform operations based on permissions granted by the administrator. They can create, modify, and delete objects in VxFlex Manager where they have been given access.
  • Read-only users can view VxFlex Manager objects, but cannot create, modify, or delete any of those objects.

Refer to About roles for detailed information.
